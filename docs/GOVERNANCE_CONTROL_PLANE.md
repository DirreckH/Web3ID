# Governance Control Plane

Phase4 governance expands the operator plane without letting it bypass frozen semantics.

## Roles

- `viewer`
- `analyst`
- `operator`
- `recovery_operator`
- `governance_reviewer`
- `auditor`
- `admin`

## Governed Actions

- recovery execution
- break-glass
- positive uplift
- policy exception
- cross-chain consume

## Rules

- approvals are explicit tickets
- dual approval can be required
- AI suggestions never replace human approval
- approvals are auditable and versioned
- break-glass cannot perform raw state rewrite

## Positive Uplift

Positive uplift remains a first-class path in Phase4:

- trust boost
- unlock eligibility
- capability restore
- positive badge or reputation-oriented review

These still require explanation, approval, and audit where applicable.
